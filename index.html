  • about us:

    Rensselaer Gaming Alliance is for gamers of all types and backgrounds to get together and play the games they love!
    We host two, single-game lan tournaments each semester, which lead into our biggest event of the semester: The UberLAN! Our ÜberLAN is a massive 12 hour event with tournaments in over 10 of the most popular video games as decided by our membership. Each event will have its own prize pool with prizes from all kinds of companies in the gaming community such as Valve. So come blast away your friends for fun and prizes!
