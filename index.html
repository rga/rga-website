  • about us:

    Rensselaer Gaming Alliance is for gamers of all types and backgrounds to get together and play the games they love. We host biweekly lan parties with console games and a server set up for games such as Team Fortress 2, Left 4 Dead 2, and anything else you want to play. Get to know the people behind your team’s screen names and crush the teams of people you just met. Never again will you have to worry about obnoxious noobs and team griefing because at an RGA lan party you know who you’re getting into a game with. Plus, who doesn’t love free stuff? We offer prizes for mini tournaments so come blast away your friends for fun and prizes!
    Join us this semester for our amazing and epic UBER-LAN party where we host tournaments for both console and steam multiplayer games for huge prizes including the newest games, hardware, and merchandise. Not amazing at games? No worries!! You can still come to play with everyone else and enter your name for awesome door prizes!
