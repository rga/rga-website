RGA Überlan - Fall 2011

RPI's home for PC and console gamers.

  • Überlan details:

    The Fall 2011 Überlan will be held on Friday, November 11th, from 7:00pm to 7:00am in the McNeil Room at the Union.
    There will be a $10 entrance fee that covers unlimited tournament entry and food. An RPI ID is not required for entry.

  • Tournament Schedule:

    Regarding the tournaments that begin at 8pm, signups will be open until 10pm to allow people attending the hockey game to play.
    • 8:00 pm - League of Legends
    • 8:00 pm - Super Smash Bros: Brawl
    • 8:00 pm - Marvel vs. Capcom 3
    • 8:00 pm - Guitar Hero
    • 11:00 pm - Starcraft 2
    • Midnight - Team Fortress 2
    • Midnight - Halo: Reach
    • 1:00 am - Counter Strike: Source
